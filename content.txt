Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in 
Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest 
in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a 
peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
